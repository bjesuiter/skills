## Sections Explained

- **Context**: The situation that prompted this decision
- **Decision**: What was decided (use active voice)
- **Alternatives**: Other options considered with pros/cons
- **Consequences**: Outcomes (positive and negative)
- **Related Decisions**: Links to dependent or related ADRs
